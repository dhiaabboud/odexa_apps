What is Dropshipping?
Dropshipping involves three participants/parties:
  • The customer who places the order - You odoo Website Customers
  • The seller accepts the order and brings it to the company/supplier - You as website admin.
  • The company who have the goods and deliver them- Here Aliexpress
Introducing Aliexpress Dropshipping in Odoo
  • You import Aliexpress Website product Data to your Odoo website so you can accept the customers orders for Aliexpress products
  • Then, send the orders to Aliexpress and Aliexpress delivers the product directly to the customers.
  • You do not need to manage, store or deliver the product to customers.

Import Desired Product Data as Feeds From Aliexpress Website to Odoo

  • You can choose what product data to import from respective Odoo settings.
  • You can enable/disable data import such as description, price, etc.
  • You can also set the price of the imported product as the same, higher or lower than on Aliexpress to add margins

Auto-Create Products From Imported Product Feeds

  • There are two options to auto-create products from product feed - instantly or using scheduler.
  • The scheduler comes configured with the module.
  • Alternatively, Instant auto-created can be enabled from settings.
  • If instant auto-created is disbaled, the scheduler creates the products at regular intervals.

Auto-Fetch Product updates from Aliexpress Website to Odoo

  • The module can automatically fetch updates of created products from Aliexpress.
  • The module comes with two default configured schedulers to update the product data.
  • One Cron updates the current price and quantity from Aliexpress to Odoo. (fast update)
  • Another scheduler updates all the product data such as description, pictures, etc.
